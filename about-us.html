Our Story

Afro Urban Hub is a vibrant community of professionals, activists, and stakeholders who are passionate about urban development and sustainability in Africa. Our members come from diverse backgrounds, including academia, research and development, technology and innovation, planning and design, consulting, policy, advocacy, and more. Together, we share knowledge, collaborate on innovative solutions, and drive positive change in our cities.

Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment where every member can contribute to and benefit from the collective efforts of the community. By joining AUH, you become part of a transformative journey to make African cities more sustainable and livable for everyone

What we do

What we do

Afro Urban Hub provides technical and advisory services to local governments, focusing on inclusive urban planning, energy efficiency, green building, sustainability, and project management. Acting as a collaboration hub, AUH connects governments, innovators, investors, and interest groups to address urban challenges through community-driven solutions. They offer scalable technological solutions to improve urban life and promote sustainable development initiatives that reduce environmental impact and enhance community well-being.

Technical and Advisory Services

We provide technical and advisory services to local governments, focusing on inclusive urban planning, energy efficiency, green building construction, sustainability, and project planning and management. Our expertise helps local governments create more livable and sustainable urban environments.

Hub for Collaboration

AUH serves as a central hub connecting local governments, inventors, innovators, investors, and other interest groups. We facilitate collaboration and knowledge exchange to find effective solutions to urban challenges. By bringing together diverse stakeholders, we foster a community-driven approach to urban development.

Technological Solutions

We offer technological solutions to address urban problems, leveraging innovation to improve the quality of life in African cities. Our solutions are designed to be practical and scalable, ensuring that they can be implemented effectively to meet the unique needs of each community

Sustainable Development Initiatives

We promote and advance green building practices and sustainable neighborhood development. Our initiatives aim to reduce the environmental impact of urbanization while enhancing the social and economic well-being of communities. Through education, advocacy, and practical projects, we drive sustainable development in African cities.
