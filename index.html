Afro Urban Hub

Transforming African Cities through Integrated Urban Solutions and Sustainable Practices

Mission

Our mission is to promote an integrative approach to addressing urban challenges in Africa by fostering collaboration among urban actors and interest groups. We aim to advance green building practices and sustainable neighborhood development to create more resilient, inclusive, and sustainable cities and communities.

Vision

Our vision is to transform African cities into thriving, sustainable urban centers where diverse stakeholders work together holistically to address urbanization challenges. We envision cities that are resilient, inclusive, and prosperous, offering a high quality of life for all residents.

Our Core values

Ingegrative Planning

AUH values a comprehensive and integrative approach to addressing urban challenges. Instead of working in isolation, AUH promotes collaboration among urban actors and interest groups to view and solve urban issues from a 360Â° perspective.

Community and Collaboration

AUH fosters a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about urban development and sustainability. By joining AUH, members can connect, collaborate, and share knowledge, working together to drive positive change.

Resilience and Inclusivity

AUH is dedicated to making cities and communities more resilient and inclusive. The organization believes in creating environments that can withstand challenges while ensuring that all community members are included and considered in urban planning and development processes.

Sustainable Practices

AUH promotes green building practices and sustainable neighborhood development. The organization is committed to advancing sustainable solutions that reduce environmental impact and enhance the quality of life in urban areas.

Innovation and Knowledge Sharing

AUH values innovation and the exchange of ideas. The organization encourages members to innovate, share their expertise, and adopt best practices to address urban challenges effectively and create a brighter future for African cities.
